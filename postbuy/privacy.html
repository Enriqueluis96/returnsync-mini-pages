Support Data safety

Privacy policy

No customer, order, checkout, cart, or payment data is stored.

PostBuy AI Upsell Mini stores only Shopify session state, merchant offer configuration, billing metadata, aggregate monthly usage counters, and non-sensitive technical status.

Data handling

  • Checkout extension requests are authenticated by Shopify.
  • Only non-personal checkout signals are processed ephemerally.
  • Optional AI receives no names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, or full payloads.
  • Prompts and AI responses are not persisted.
  • Support contact: support@b2bmerchanttools.com
