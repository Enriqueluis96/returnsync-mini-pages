Shopify mini-app

Flag refund events that may need subscription follow-up.

ReturnSync Mini watches Shopify refund events, checks subscription signals at processing time, and applies merchant-controlled follow-up when a safe subscription target is available.

Zero-data posture

No customer, order, refund, return, sales reversal, or subscription records are stored.

Conservative workflow

Follow-up modes are merchant controlled, with automatic cancellation behind a separate opt-in guard.

Simple billing

Free includes 100 refund events per month. Pro is $9 USD every 30 days through Shopify Billing.

Built for Shopify review clarity

  • Embedded Shopify Remix app.
  • Shopify Admin GraphQL API 2026-04.
  • Compliance webhooks for data request, customer redact, and shop redact.
  • Webhook processing with no payload persistence.
  • Optional AI exchange suggestion with deterministic fallback.
