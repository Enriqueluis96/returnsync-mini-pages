Privacy Policy

Privacy by default for refund and subscription follow-up.

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Data We Store

ReturnSync Mini stores only merchant and operational metadata:

  • Shopify OAuth/session state required to operate the embedded app.
  • Merchant configuration, such as provider, action mode, AI toggle, and billing plan.
  • Shopify Billing metadata needed to determine Free or Pro status.
  • Aggregated monthly refund event counters for plan limits.
  • Latest non-sensitive technical webhook status, such as action code, reason code, provider status, and timestamp.

Data We Do Not Store

  • Customer records.
  • Customer names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses.
  • Order records.
  • Refund payloads.
  • Return payloads.
  • Sales reversal data.
  • Subscription data.
  • Full webhook payloads.
  • AI prompts or AI responses containing personal or sensitive operational data.

Webhook Processing

When Shopify sends a webhook, ReturnSync Mini authenticates it, extracts only minimum identifiers, queries only minimal Shopify Admin GraphQL context if processing is active, applies deterministic fallback behavior when needed, logs only non-sensitive technical status, and discards the payload.

AI Usage

Optional AI exchange suggestions use only non-personal operational signals. Customer names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, full order data, full return reasons, webhook payloads, and subscription records are not sent to AI providers.

Data Retention

Merchant configuration, Shopify session state, billing metadata, aggregated counters, and latest non-sensitive technical status are retained while the app is installed. Shop redact and uninstall webhooks delete merchant configuration, Shopify sessions, and usage metadata.
