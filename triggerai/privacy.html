TriggerAI Bar Mini
Support Data safety

Privacy policy

No customer profiles, cart history, orders, or behavior history are stored.

TriggerAI Bar Mini stores only Shopify session state, merchant bar configuration, billing metadata, aggregate monthly counters, and non-sensitive technical status.

Data handling

  • Storefront requests are authenticated through Shopify App Proxy.
  • Only aggregate signals are processed ephemerally.
  • Optional AI receives page type, cart item count, subtotal band, time band, and scroll band.
  • Prompts and AI responses are not persisted.
